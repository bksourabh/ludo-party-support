Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play online with friends?

To play online, you need to sign in with Game Center. From the main menu, select "Create Online Game" to host a match or "Join Online Game" to find existing matches. Your friends will need Game Center as well.

Can I save my game and continue later?

Yes! Offline games are automatically saved after each turn. When you return to the app, select "Continue Game" from the main menu to resume your saved game.

How do I change players between Human and Computer?

When starting a new offline game, tap on any player color (Red, Green, Yellow, Blue) to toggle between Human and Computer control.

What are the game rules?

Ludo Party follows classic Ludo rules:

  • Roll a 6 to bring a token out of your starting yard
  • Move tokens clockwise around the board based on dice rolls
  • Land on an opponent's token to capture it (sends it back to their yard)
  • Rolling a 6, capturing a token, or reaching home gives you a bonus roll
  • Three consecutive 6s void your turn
  • First player to get all 4 tokens home wins!

Why can't I move my token?

You can only move tokens that have a valid move. If all your tokens are in the yard, you need to roll a 6 to bring one out. Tokens that can move will be highlighted.

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at support@helpingthoughtgames.com.au with:

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce (if possible)
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Screenshots (if applicable)

Is the game free?

Yes! Ludo Party is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases.
