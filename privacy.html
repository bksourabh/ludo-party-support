Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Helping Thought Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Simple Ludo mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Summary: We respect your privacy. Simple Ludo collects minimal data and does not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Information Collection and Use

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our App. The types of information we may collect include:

Information We Collect

Game Center Data (Optional)

  • Display name for online multiplayer functionality
  • Player identifier for matchmaking

This information is collected and managed by Apple's Game Center service when you choose to use online multiplayer features.

Sign in with Apple (Optional)

  • Apple ID identifier
  • Name (if you choose to share it)
  • Email (if you choose to share it)

This information is only collected if you choose to sign in with Apple. You can also use the app as a guest without signing in.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data
  • Contact information from your device
  • Photos or media
  • Browsing history
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Financial information

Data Storage

Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not store any personal data on external servers. Online multiplayer data is handled entirely by Apple's Game Center service.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Game Center: For online multiplayer features. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Sign in with Apple: For optional user authentication. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

We value your trust in using our App. While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information. Since we store data locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally in the App
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the App
  • Opt out of Game Center by signing out in your device settings
  • Use the App without signing in (as a guest)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
